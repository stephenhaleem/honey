ABOUT

THE ARTIST BEHIND THE LENS

I am a portrait photographer based in New York City with a passion for capturing the authentic essence of every subject. My work focuses on creating intimate portraits that reveal both strength and vulnerability.

With over 8 years of experience in portrait photography, I've developed a style that blends fine art sensibilities with editorial flair. My approach combines technical precision with emotional intuition, allowing me to create images that resonate on a deeper level.

My work has been featured in various publications and galleries across the country. I've had the privilege of collaborating with individuals from all walks of life, each bringing their unique story to the frame.
